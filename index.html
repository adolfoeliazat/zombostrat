The Postapocalyptic Zombie Survival Game set in your neighborhood.

This game uses Google Maps to populate a zombiescape for you to explore, loot and eventually die in! What fun!

Help

The goal of Corpseburg is to survive until day 30s of quarantine in a location of your choosing, then make it to an extraction point that will randomly appear in one of the 4 corners of the map. To do this you will need to scavenge food, weapons, and defensive supplies from your surroundings. Using the scout button will locate local shops in your area that might have some supplies. Restaurants are likely to have food, factories are a great place to find defensive supplies. Explore your surroundings while avoiding the undead. If you do get injured, you can use medicine to heal at night. Speaking of night, keep an eye on the clock, when the sun goes down it becomes much harder to avoid the hordes of zombies. You should prepare a fortified camp to sleep in each night.

Game Over!

Death has claimed you. If you can make it to day 30 and make it to the evac point, you win! Any achievements you earned during gameplay will make future attempts easier.

As you play the game, you will unlock achievements which will change how you play the game on your next run through. Achievements can either give you additional starting items, or increase your stats. Here is a quick summary of what your stats do in game:

  • Strength: Increases the damage you do in combat, making you less likely to take damage from zombies.
  • Dexterity: Decrease the amount of time certain scouting actions take.
  • Constitution: Increases the amount you heal at night.
  • Intelligence: Increases the effect your defensive preparations have.
  • Wits: Helps you search more quickly, may help you find better locations to loot.
